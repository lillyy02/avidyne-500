Avidyne 500 Map Update – Complete Update Instructions

The Avidyne 500 series is a powerful avionics suite used widely in general aviation. Updating the map and navigation database is essential to maintain flight safety, accuracy, and regulatory compliance. This article will guide you through the Avidyne 500 map update process step-by-step.

Why Update the Avidyne 500 Maps?

  • Ensure current navigation data including airways, airports, and navaids
  • Comply with FAA and ICAO requirements
  • Enhance in-flight situational awareness
  • Improve terrain and obstacle alerting accuracy

What You’ll Need

  • Avidyne-approved USB flash drive (4–16 GB, FAT32 format)
  • Access to the aircraft and avionics unit
  • Active data subscription (Jeppesen or Avidyne)
  • JDM software or Avidyne Data Loader installed on your PC
  • Stable internet connection

How to Update Avidyne 500 Maps

  1. Download and install the Jeppesen JDM or Avidyne Data Loader software.
  2. Insert the USB drive into your PC and launch the update software.
  3. Select your Avidyne 500 system and download the latest database.
  4. Safely eject the USB once the download is complete.
  5. Power on your aircraft’s avionics in update/maintenance mode.
  6. Insert the USB drive into the Avidyne 500 unit’s USB port.
  7. Follow on-screen prompts to begin the update process.
  8. Wait for installation to finish and verify the update version afterward.

Tips & Troubleshooting

  • Always verify your subscription matches the 500 series platform.
  • Format USB drives in FAT32 to avoid errors.
  • Never power off the avionics during the update process.
  • If errors occur, redownload the update or try a different USB stick.

Conclusion

Regular updates to the Avidyne 500 system ensure safer navigation, better system performance, and up-to-date flight planning. Use this guide before every update cycle to avoid common pitfalls and maintain compliance.
